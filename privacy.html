Privacy Policy

RD Exam Prep: Dietitian CDR · Last updated 14 July 2026

The short version. RD Exam Prep: Dietitian CDR does not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data. There is no account, no login, no analytics SDK and no advertising SDK. Everything you do in the app stays on your device.

1. Who we are

RD Exam Prep: Dietitian CDR ("the app") is published by MD Shahariar Nazim Joy ("we", "us"). You can reach us at dmjks120@gmail.com.

2. Data we collect

None. We do not collect personal data of any kind. Specifically, the app does not collect your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, location, device identifiers, advertising identifiers, IP address, or any usage or diagnostic analytics.

The app contains no third-party analytics frameworks, no advertising frameworks, no crash-reporting SDK, and no tracking technologies as defined by Apple's App Tracking Transparency framework. We do not track you across apps or websites owned by other companies, and we do not ask for permission to do so, because we do not do it.

3. Data stored on your device

The app stores the following locally on your iPhone or iPad so it can work as a study tool. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us:

  • Your answers, per-question statistics, and spaced-repetition schedule
  • Bookmarked questions and flashcard progress
  • Mock exam results and your study streak
  • Your onboarding answers: study goal, study focus, exam date, focus areas, daily question goal, and reminder time

You can erase all of it at any time from Settings → Reset all progress, or by deleting the app. Deleting the app removes this data from your device permanently.

4. Notifications

If you turn on the daily study reminder, the app schedules a local notification on your device using Apple's notification framework. Local notifications are generated and delivered entirely on your device. No reminder content, schedule, or device token is sent to us or to any server. You can turn reminders off in the app's Settings tab, or in the iOS Settings app, at any time.

5. Purchases

RD Exam Prep: Dietitian CDR is a paid app and requires a subscription or the lifetime unlock to use. All purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store using StoreKit. Your payment details, Apple Account information, and billing history are handled entirely by Apple and are never seen by, sent to, or stored by us. We receive no personally identifiable purchase information. The app verifies your entitlement on-device against Apple's signed transaction records so it knows whether to unlock. Apple's handling of purchase data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

6. Network use

The app is offline-first. The entire question bank is bundled inside the app. The app connects to the network only for two things: to talk to Apple's App Store servers about your purchase and entitlements, and if you tap a link that opens a website in your browser (e.g. a source link in Exam Info). No other network requests are made.

7. Children

The app is a professional exam-preparation tool intended for adults studying for a Registered Dietitian credential. It is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Your rights

Because we hold no personal data about you, there is nothing for us to disclose, correct, export, or delete on your behalf. All of your study data is under your control on your own device, and you can delete it at any time as described in section 3. If you are in the EU/EEA, the UK, or California, the rights those laws give you (access, deletion, portability, opting out of sale) apply to data a company holds about you - and we hold none.

9. Data retention and security

We retain no data, so there is no retention period. Data on your device is protected by iOS and by your device passcode, Face ID, or Touch ID.

10. Changes to this policy

If this policy ever changes, we will post the revised version at this URL and update the date at the top. If a future version of the app were ever to begin collecting data, we would say so here clearly and in advance, and would ask for your consent where the law requires it.

11. Contact

Questions about this policy? Email dmjks120@gmail.com. We reply to every message.
